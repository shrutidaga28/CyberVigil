Predictive Analytics

Welcome to the Predictive Analytics section. Here, you can explore various aspects of predictive analytics related to cyber threats and fraud detection.

2023

The year 2023 saw the highest levels of cyber threats over the past five years. Phishing attacks and malware infections continued to rise, with ransomware incidents reaching 20,000 and DDoS attacks hitting 30,000. Data breaches and insider threats also saw substantial increases.

2022

In 2022, cyber threats continued to escalate, with phishing attacks reaching 65,000 and malware infections surpassing 50,000. Ransomware incidents and DDoS attacks also saw significant growth.

2021

The trend of increasing cyber threats continued in 2021, with significant rises in phishing attacks, malware infections, and ransomware incidents. Data breaches and insider threats also saw increases.

2020

In 2020, there was an increase in all types of cyber threats compared to 2019. Phishing attacks and malware infections continued to dominate, with noticeable increases in ransomware incidents and DDoS attacks.
